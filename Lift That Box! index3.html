Statement of Informed Consent

Please consider this information carefully before deciding whether to participate in this experiment.

Purpose of the research: To examine the way people make judgments and decisions.

What you will do in this research: Text and images will be presented on the computer and you will be asked to answer questions.

Time required: Participation will take about 10 minutes to complete.

Risks: There are no anticipated risks associated with participating in this study. The effects of participating should be comparable to those you would experience from viewing a computer monitor for the task duration and using a mouse or keyboard.

Benefits: This study provides no benefits to you individually. The study provides important information about how people make decisions. Others may benefit in the future from the results of this study.

Compensation: You will receive $0.5 base payment and a potential bonus payment up to $3.00 via Amazon Mechanical Turk for completing the entire experiment.

Confidentiality: Your participation in this study will remain confidential. No personally identifiable information will be associated with your data. Your de-identified data may be shared with other researchers and used in future projects.

Participation and withdrawal: Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you may refuse to participate or you may choose to withdraw at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

How to contact the researchers: If you have questions or concerns about your participation or payment, or want to request a summary of research findings, please contact Sam Gershman, gershman@fas.harvard.edu, (617) 496-6655.

Whom to contact about your rights as a participant in this research: For questions, concerns, suggestions, or complaints that have not been or cannot be addressed by the researcher, or to report research-related harm, please contact the Committee on the Use of Human Subjects at Harvard University, 1414 Massachusetts Avenue, Second Floor, Cambridge, MA 02138; Email: cuhs@harvard.edu; Phone: (617) 496-2847.
